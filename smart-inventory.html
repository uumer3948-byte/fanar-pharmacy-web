Smart Inventory: Automated Stock & Expiry Tracking

Managing a pharmacy requires extreme precision. With Fanar Pharmacy's Smart Inventory system, you no longer have to rely on manual stock taking. Our software handles the heavy lifting for you.

Step 1: Automated Barcode Scanning

Utilize our advanced system to automatically scan medicines into your database. By scanning the barcode, the system instantly logs the medicine name, batch number, and essential details into your central server.

Step 2: Real-time Stock Updates

Every time a medication is billed at the POS, the inventory updates in real-time. This prevents stock shortages and ensures you always know exactly what is available on your shelves.

Step 3: Proactive Expiry Alerts

Never lose money on expired medications again. The system actively monitors expiry dates and sends dashboard alerts 30, 60, and 90 days before a batch expires, allowing you to return or discount items proactively.
