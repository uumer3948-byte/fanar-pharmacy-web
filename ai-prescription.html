AI Prescription Scan: Instant Medicine Detection

Deciphering doctor handwriting is a thing of the past. Our state-of-the-art AI Prescription Scanning tool uses advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to automate your dispensing process.

Smart OCR Technology

Simply upload a scanned image or take a clear photo of the patient's prescription. Our AI engine is trained on thousands of medical terms and doctors' handwriting samples to accurately detect the prescribed medicines in seconds.

Automatic Cart Population

Once the AI reads the prescription, it immediately cross-references the required medicines with your Smart Inventory. If the stock is available, the items are automatically added to the billing cart with the prescribed dosages attached.

Digital Record Keeping

Keep your pharmacy compliant effortlessly. Every scanned prescription is securely saved and linked to the patient's profile and invoice, making future audits or refill requests incredibly simple.
