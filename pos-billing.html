POS Billing: Fast & Accurate Checkout

Speed up your pharmacy checkout line and eliminate manual calculation errors. Our Point of Sale (POS) system is designed specifically for the fast-paced retail pharmacy environment.

Lightning-Fast Transactions

With seamless barcode scanner integration, simply scan the medicine, and it's instantly added to the cart. The system automatically pulls the current price, batch number, and applicable discounts in milliseconds.

Integrated GST & Tax Support

No more complex tax math. Our software automatically calculates the correct GST tiers for different types of medications and non-pharma products, generating fully compliant invoices ready for printing or emailing to the customer.

Multiple Payment Modes

Give your customers flexibility. Easily split bills across different payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets, all tracked meticulously in your daily sales reports.
